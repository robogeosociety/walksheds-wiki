# Issaquah–South Kirkland Link

!!! warning "Stub article"
    This is a placeholder for a planned line. Details and dates will be filled in as Sound Transit's plans firm up.

A planned **new Eastside line**, separate from the [2 Line](line-2.md), running from **Issaquah** through Bellevue to **South Kirkland**, connecting to the 2 Line in downtown Bellevue.

**Status:** in early planning, projected for the 2040s — the furthest-out of the ST3 projects.

**Why it matters:** it would give the central Eastside its own north–south rail spine and tie fast-growing Issaquah and Kirkland into the network.

*See the [future lines overview](future.md) for the rest of the planned network.*
