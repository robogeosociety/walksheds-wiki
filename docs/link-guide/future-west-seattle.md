# West Seattle Link

!!! warning "Stub article"
    This is a placeholder for a planned line. Details and dates will be filled in as Sound Transit's plans firm up.

A planned extension carrying light rail from downtown Seattle, through SODO and across the Duwamish River, to **West Seattle** — ending near the Alaska Junction, with stops planned around Delridge and Avalon.

**Status:** in design and early construction, planned to open in the early 2030s.

**Why it matters:** West Seattle is a large, hilly peninsula whose only connections today are a handful of bridges and bus routes. Rail would give the neighborhood a fast, reliable spine — and new walksheds around each station.
