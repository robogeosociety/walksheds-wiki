A planned line running north from the Chinatown-International District through a **new downtown transit tunnel** — adding stations in South Lake Union and near Seattle Center — and on to **Ballard**.

**Status:** in planning and design, with opening projected for the late 2030s.

**Why it matters:** the new tunnel doubles downtown's rail capacity and threads through some of Seattle's densest, fastest-growing neighborhoods — South Lake Union, Uptown, Interbay, Ballard — each a rich walkshed waiting for a station.
