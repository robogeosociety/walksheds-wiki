# Future lines

Link is still growing. The 2016 voter-approved **ST3** package funds a wave of new lines and extensions that will reshape the map over the coming decades, and a few infill stations will fill gaps in the track that's already running.

These pages are **stubs** — short placeholders to be filled in as plans firm up. Dates are deliberately vague; Sound Transit's timelines have moved more than once.

## Planned extensions and new lines

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

## Infill stations

New stops squeezed into the *existing* line where the gaps are largest — a cheap way to add walksheds to track that's already running:

- **NE 130th Street** — between Northgate and Shoreline South, the furthest along.
- **Graham Street** — in the Rainier Valley, between Othello and Columbia City.
- **Boeing Access Road** — in the south, near the Tukwila/Seattle line.

When any of these opens, its walkshed simply appears on the [map](https://walksheds.xyz) — another neighborhood brought within a 15-minute walk of the train.
